# Privacy Policy for Football 501

**Last updated: 27 April 2026**

This policy explains what data Football 501 collects and how it is used.

## What we collect

Football 501 does not collect any personal information.

The app does not require an account, sign-up, or login. We do not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifying information.

## Network requests

When you use Football 501, the app fetches football statistics (player names, club records, goal and appearance totals) from our own server. These requests contain no personal information about you. We do not log who is making the requests in any way that could identify an individual user.

## Game data

All game state — scores, current rounds, who is winning — is stored only on your device. Nothing about your gameplay is sent to us or to any third party.

## Data shared with third parties

None.

## Children

Football 501 is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Analytics and crash reporting

The current version of Football 501 does not include analytics or crash reporting tools. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated and the change will be noted in the app's release notes.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change what the app collects, we will update this page with the new date at the top and describe what changed.
